About Me

I am a master student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. My research areas are cyber security and data science, specifically, how to use data analysis and artificial intelligence techniques to discover and diagnose potential security risks in systems.

I received my BSc in Computer Science with Sexton Distinction (1st Class Distinction) from Dalhousie University (DAL), Halifax, NS, Canada in 2021.

After graduation, I worked on Android ROM development, focusing on wireless application development and Framework development (LE_Audio, Miracast).
